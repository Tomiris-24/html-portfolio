The Fate of the Furious

Genre: Action, Crime, Thriller

Plot: Dominic Toretto turns against his team under the influence of Cipher, a cyberterrorist. His family must stop global destruction with high-speed chases and thrilling stunts.

The Hunger Games: Catching Fire

Genre: Action, Sci-Fi, Adventure

Plot: Katniss Everdeen returns for the 75th Hunger Games (Quarter Quell), facing new dangers and becoming the symbol of rebellion against the Capitol.

Fun Fact: The "Hanging Tree" song became a real-life chart hit!

The Intouchables

Genre: Comedy, Drama, Biography

Plot: A wealthy quadriplegic and his street-smart caregiver form an unlikely but heartwarming friendship filled with humor and life lessons.

Fun Fact: The film inspired several remakes, including "The Upside" starring Kevin Hart.
